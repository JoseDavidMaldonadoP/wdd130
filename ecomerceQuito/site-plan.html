Overview

Purpose

The fundamental idea of the project is to create an e-commerce store for the city of Quito, where we will connect multiple professional services and entrepreneurial businesses with their future clients through the website which will allow the supplier to register their product, brand and / or service with a subscription that will allow you to access advertising and product delivery facilities to your customers.

Audience

Our audience is every entrepreneur, or independent professional service, or medium-sized business, that is developing an idea or business but does not have the facility to advertise their product on the web and/or cannot manage their business digitally.

Content

Home page

We present an online store capable of offering and presenting the professional services of people, or companies that are starting a business in our country and especially in our city, thus providing the possibility of connecting suppliers with their future clients through our online store. that provides timely management in the logistics of product delivery, if the customer does not know, does not have the resource or does not know how to do it, and also our online store allows payment through digital means, thus doing this transaction, a very safe option against the insecurity that is beginning in our environment due to crime and extortion that has grown in the streets of our country and especially in each city of Ecuador. How many times have we wondered how I find my future Clients? The answer is simple since we are in a digitized world where the first option to obtain or learn about something is to look for it on the Web, it is logical that if we want to offer our professional services and/or offer products generated by our businesses, we must look for the way to place it in the virtual showcases available to everyone and even specifically within the reach of our community. Our Online Store will allow our customers and affiliates to have the possibility of presenting their products and/or services on our website with the possibility of making updates to their product gallery with parameter information that will allow them to know which products are the most selected in your business that is published in our online store, have access to real-time comments of what people or future customers are looking for the most so that said information can be used by the supplier and make the necessary decisions for when to stock products and the rest. And finally our online store will have a presence on social networks such as facebook, twitter, Instagram with a wassap interface allowing real-time communication from our online store to our suppliers. Control the products selected in the cart of the online store in order to take into account which product is most in demand and the possibility of saving this selection so that on the next visit the customer does not have to look for the product again. To publish your business and/or professional service in our online store, our future associates must read and accept our affiliation contract term and subscribe to our affiliate page where we will present all the benefits that include being part of our online store. This line is digital marketing advice focused on your needs, such as the requirements of adequate or professional photos to be published, in the case of businesses, photos of the premises and/or products offered by your business would be needed, or in the case of independent professionals, it would be necessary Presentable profile photos that are needed to be published in the Professional Services section. All of the above will make it possible for your business or service to be more visible on the Web, since our online store presents a practically new idea, which is the fusion of products with professional services of all kinds that will work together in order to meet your needs. needs require, for example, if a business or store published on our website has the need to ship a product and does not have the transportation to do so, one of our affiliates and/or professional service partners that their service is in the area of transportation, could make the delivery thus showing a system of mutual support between suppliers and clients.
